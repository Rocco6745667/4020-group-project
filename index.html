Welcome to Our Final Project Webpage

Welcome to our ITEC 4020 Final Project, a comprehensive exploration of web development concepts interwoven with a focused analysis of ChatGPT's efficiency across multiple domains.

In this project, we have crafted an engaging and informative website that highlights our research findings, presents insightful data visualizations, and evaluates the performance and practical applications of ChatGPT. This project aims to merge technical skills in front-end web design with data-driven analysis. By navigating through our portfolio, you will experience interactive elements, modern design practices, and a thorough examination of ChatGPT's capabilities.

Through this assignment, we demonstrate our proficiency in handling web-based APIs, designing intuitive interfaces, and communicating complex technical insights effectively. Enjoy exploring our work, and we hope you find our findings both enlightening and impactful!
